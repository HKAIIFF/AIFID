FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

AIF (AI Film Token) is the native utility token of the HKAIIFF ecosystem. It powers all transactions within our AI film platforms including 404.world, AIF.Market, and AIF.Exchange. AIF is required for watching films, investing in FilmTokens, staking for rewards, and participating in DAO governance.

Watch-to-Earn is our revolutionary model where users earn AIF tokens by watching films on 404.world. The reward pool of 4 billion AIF tokens is distributed over 5 years. Your rewards are calculated based on watch time, content quality coefficient, and user weight. For example, watching a 90-minute film could earn you 108 AIF tokens.

FilmTokens are project-specific tokens representing ownership and revenue rights in individual AI film projects. When you invest in a FilmToken on AIF.Market, you receive a share of the film's box office revenue (typically 50-60% of total revenue goes to token holders). FilmTokens can also be traded on the secondary market at AIF.Exchange.

We offer three staking options: Flexible (0-day lockup): 45% APY, 90-day lockup: 75% APY, and 365-day lockup: 120% APY. Staking rewards come from three sources: 40% from new token releases, 30% from platform fee dividends, and 30% from ecosystem revenue distribution. For example, staking 10,000 AIF for 365 days at 120% APY would earn you 12,000 AIF in rewards.

AIF has a fixed total supply of 10 billion tokens with no additional minting. We implement two burn mechanisms: 20% of all platform transaction fees are permanently burned, and 50% of platform profits are used to buyback and burn AIF tokens. We project burning 5.1% of total supply (510 million AIF) over the first 5 years, with actual circulating supply decreasing to 9.49 billion tokens.

AIF Token Generation Event (TGE) is scheduled for Q4 2025. The IEO/IDO will launch on major DEXs including Jupiter and Raydium on Solana. Initial price is set at $0.05 per token with 1.5 billion tokens (15% of total supply) available in the public sale. Early participants can benefit from the lowest entry price and highest potential returns.
